About Smaart™framework

SMAART™ utilizes combined principles of Data, Information and Knowledge (DIK), Human Centered approach, Cognitive Fit Theory, Information processing theory and humanistic, behavioral and learning theories. SMAART™ framework utilizes DIK pathway to make data more meaningful. Human Centered approach incorporates the users’ perspective in order to create a system that is useful and use able.
